How to cook adobo

Ingredients

  • 30 g cooking oil
  • 24 g garlic crushed
  • 30 g onion, sliced
  • 1 kilogram chicken cut ups
  • 30 g vinegar
  • 60 g soy sauce
  • 240 g water
  • 4 g bay leaves
  • 2 g whole black peppercorns, slightly crushed
  • 2 pc Knorr chicken cubes
  • 4 g brown sugar packed
  • Option: potato

How to cook

  1. Heat oil in pan and saut√© garlic and onions.
    • If you can already smell the onion and garlic then its already good.
  2. Then add chicken to the pan and sear on all sides.
    • It needs to have a little browning in the chicken skin.
  3. Pour in vinegar, soy sauce and water. Add bay leaves, pepper and Knorr Chicken Cubes. Bring to a boil over high heat then reduce heat to simmer, but do not cover the pan.
    • Simmer it for 10-15mins.
  4. Remove chicken pieces from sauce and fry in another pan until nicely browned.
  5. Put back fried chicken pieces into sauce. Add sugar and let simmer again for another 10 minutes or until sauce has thickened. Serve warm.
